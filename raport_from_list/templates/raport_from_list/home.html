Fast and Reliable Custom Report Generator

Streamline Your Document Creation Process With Just a Few Clicks

Welcome to our Custom Report Generator, a state-of-the-art application designed to simplify your report creation process. Our platform uses a unique, automated approach to dynamically generate multiple reports from a single template. No more manual editing, no more copy-pasting.

Key Features:

  1. Upload and Go: Upload a DOCX file and an Excel file with words to replace, and let our system do the rest.
  2. Manual Custom List: Generate reports from a custom list without needing to upload an Excel file. Simply input the words to replace, and their replacements, then click generate.
  3. Batch Document Generation: Generate multiple unique documents at once, saving time and ensuring accuracy.
  4. Format Flexibility: Download your generated documents as either DOCX or PDF. We also offer a convenient ZIP download for batch documents.
  5. Secure and Private: Your data is safe with us. All uploaded files and generated documents are automatically deleted after download or logout.

Whether you are working on sales proposals, personalized letters, certificates, or any document requiring repeated customizations, our Custom Report Generator can significantly streamline your work process.

File Storage Duration:

Generated files are stored on our server for one week. After that, they are automatically deleted to ensure privacy and data security.
