Łukasz Gąsior

Hello, my name is Łukasz. Currently, I work as a scientist at the Polish Academy of Sciences, specializing in reproductive biology and neurobiology. Although my academic background is in biological sciences where I earned a doctorate, my interest shifted towards IT, specifically in Python programming, web development, and data analysis.

I find great satisfaction in problem-solving and experimenting, and programming provides a platform to iterate and learn without the cost constraints of biological experiments. I've always been interested in continual growth and learning, but I found my options for development in the scientific field limited. This led me to explore another field - Information Technology.

Projects

Capybara Investments Platform: An innovative investment management platform developed using Django, providing comprehensive market analysis, investment strategy optimization, and advanced portfolio management. I integrated OpenAI's GPT technology to streamline protocol summarization for neurobiologists at the Polish Academy of Sciences.

Report Generator: As the lead developer, I designed and implemented an advanced custom report generation system.
